This is the website of the alumni of the Mississippi School for Mathematics and Science (MSMS). The MSMS Alumni Association (MSMSAA) was founded in 1994 to facilitate communication among the alumni by maintaining a database of up-to-date contact information and organizing alumni events.

Our interests and activities sometimes overlap with MSMS, MSMS Foundation and MSMS PLUS, but we are an independent organization primarily interested in supporting the alumni.
